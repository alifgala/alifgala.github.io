An SOW is not just an administrative document, it's a roadmap that provides clarity on what needs to be done, how it will be done, and when the results are expected.

Without an SOW, data projects can quickly become chaotic:

  • Misaligned expectations between the team and stakeholders.
  • Unplanned requests that arise mid-project (scope creep).
  • End results that don't meet the initial objectives.
