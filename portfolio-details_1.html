Unlocking Customer Potential with RFM Segmentation

  1. Home
  2. Portfolio Details

Project information

  • Category: Data Analyst
  • Project Type: Final Project
  • Project date: 07 December, 2024
  • Project Repository: GITHUB
  • Project Presentation: PPT

Description

    To analyze customer behavior using RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) and segment them to support marketing strategies and improve customer retention.

Approch

  • Conducted EDA to explore patterns in customer purchasing behavior.
  • Used RFM methodology to score and classify customers.
  • Visualized segments using bar and pie charts.

Outcome

  • The analysis revealed that Potential Loyalists (19.2%) are the largest segment, showing strong potential for loyalty. At Risk (17.48%) customers need attention to prevent churn, while Promising (12.99%) are newer customers with growing engagement. The smallest group, Lost Customers (4.99%), presents an opportunity for re-engagement.
  • Actionable insights for marketing campaigns and customer loyalty strategies.
